GENERAL POLICY: Livanta does not access or use your personal information for any purpose other than to provide the functionality described in the Site or as described herein. Livanta does not disclose or further transfer your personal information other than (a) at your direction when (and if) you decide to share your personal information with another person; (b) to the extent described herein; and (c) to comply with law, such as a subpoena, or in the event that Livanta’s business is transferred to a successor through a merger, sale, or other similar sale of business transaction (a “Change of Control”). Collection of Personal Information

Although much of the Site is available without registering or providing personal information, in order to take advantage of some of our Site’s features, you will be asked to provide certain personal information such as your email address, phone number(s), physical mail addresses and other similar types of information. This personal information is provided voluntarily by you in a web form, via online chat, message or when you email us and includes but is not limited to personal information collected when you:

You are not required to provide us with this personal information, however failure to do so in some cases will limit the services or Site functions available to you.

The Site uses cookie technology which stores small files on your computer, to assist with session management, preserving your login context, and keeping track of such actions while on the Site such as referring source, domain name, internet protocol address, the type of web browser, and other data that is automatically transferred between computers in a typical internet session (“Automatically Collected Data”). Automatically Collected Data is used by Livanta only for its internal purposes but is subject to disclosure to the extent required by applicable law, or in a Change of Control. You also have choices with respect to limiting some Automatically Collected Data. For example, by modifying your browser preferences, or the settings on your electronic device that you use to access the Site, you may set an option to accept or reject cookies or to be notified when a cookie is set. Some of these setting may interfere with, or prevent the operation of the Site. Accepting a cookie in no way gives Livanta access to your computer or any personal information about you, other than the data stored in the cookies.

Anonymized/aggregated data.

If the Site de-identifies any data or information (including any personal information), or the information is itself already anonymous (i.e. most Automatically Collected Data), such that such data does not link to or identify you, then such data is anonymous data, and the Site owns this anonymous data and the Site may use such data for any purpose and disclose such data to any third-party and the Site has no duty to you in respect of such anonymous data, except solely to the extent provided under law applicable to the Site.

Livanta LLC may utilize online identification technologies from marketing partners on our sites, third party sites, and social media platforms. We use this technology to, in part, compile aggregated statistics about visitors who interact with Livanta LLC online content and to provide more pertinent information to our visitors.

Livanta LLC also uses site analytics providers which may set cookies in your browser. For example, we partner with Google Analytics, which uses Cookies to track your interactions with our Site.
